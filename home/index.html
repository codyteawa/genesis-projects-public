ABOUT US.

We are a Christchurch based consultancy providing project management services throughout New Zealand. We specialize in capital infrastructure projects, supporting project owners and customers in developing capital investment systems, planning and resourcing of projects, and in managing projects and programmes.

Objectives.

A significant proportion of organisational objectives are delivered through project based activities.

Application.

The application of both tried and innovative project management techniques can significantly improve the likelihood of an organisation achieving its strategic objectives.

One Team.

Projects are best delivered when the project management team and the project owner's organisation work as one team.

Principles.

These principles can be applied to a wide range of projects, delivering organisational benefits of sustainability, innovation and growth.

Consequently, we specialize in the role of project owner’s project manager and pride ourselves on our agile approach and ability to collaborate across all project stakeholders and supporters including senior management, engineering and operations teams, consultants and contractors. We have the ability to develop innovative solutions when required or to execute the common practices with uncommon excellence.

We have discovered that project management excellence requires the right balance between process and results driven approaches – too much process will suffocate the project and too little will derail it. There is no fixed prescription to finding this balance and it varies from project to project and throughout the project lifecycle. Getting it right takes the type of skill which Genesis Projects has developed through years of experience.
